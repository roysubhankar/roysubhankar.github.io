Contact & Opportunities

📩 Contact email (click to copy email)

I welcome inquiries from motivated students and researchers from academia and industry interested in research and collaboration. Please read the guidelines below before reaching out to ensure a good fit.

UniBG Bsc & Msc Students

If you are a Bsc or Msc student currently enrolled at UniBG interested in a thesis under my supervision, you are welcome to contact me provided that you:

  • Have attended or currently attending one of my courses (i.e., "Introduction to Machine Learning" or "Deep Learning"), or any course related to Artificial Intelligence in general
  • Have a satisfactory background in any of these programming languages: Python, C, C++, MATLAB, Java

In your email, please include: a short description of why you are interested and preferred thesis topics (if any)

External Students (Bsc / Msc / PhD)

I am open to collaborations, joint research projects, and thesis co-supervision requests from external students enrolled at any degree awarding higher education institution. You should have:

  • A solid background in machine learning and/or deep learning
  • Good programming skills in Python

When reaching out, please include:

  • Your CV
  • Your transcript of records from previous/current level of study (if available)
  • A summary of your previous research or projects (with links if available)
  • A brief explanation of how your interests align with my research

Prospective PhD Students

Prospective PhD students are welcome to contact me to express interest in working with me. A strong background in machine learning/deep learning and programming is expected. Prior publication experience is considered a plus.

When reaching out, please include:

  • Your CV
  • Your Msc transcript (unofficial is fine)
  • A summary of your previous research or projects (with links if available)
  • A brief explanation of how your interests align with my research

For formal application procedures and timelines, refer to UniBG's relevant webpages:

  • General information: PhD Programs (🇬🇧) | Dottorati di ricerca (🇮🇹)
  • PhD degree in Artificial Intelligence for Sustainable Futures
  • PhD degree in Technology, Innovation and Management

External Organizations & Industry Collaboration

I welcome inquiries from higher education institutions, research institutions, companies, and SMEs interested in collaborative research, joint projects, grant proposals, or proof-of-concept development in areas related to my work.

Potential collaboration topics include (but are not limited to) generative modelling, multimodal learning, and trustworthy AI. Please see my publications for concrete topics.

If you are interested in working together, please reach out with:

  • A brief description of your organization
  • The problem or use case you have in mind, and the scope of the proposed collaboration

Important Notes

  • I am currently unable to offer paid internships.
  • Any funded positions or opportunities will be advertised on this webpage.
  • I may not be able to respond to inquiries that do not match the above criteria.

I look forward to hearing from students, researchers, and organizations whose interests align closely with my research.
